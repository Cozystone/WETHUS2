# WENET Product Philosophy

## One-line definition
WENET is a **relationship discovery platform** that connects lifestyle-to-lifestyle with explainable compatibility, not a swipe dating app.

## Vision
- 라이프스타일과 라이프스타일을 잇는다.
- 슬로건: 의미 있는 연결

## Product principles
1. Discovery over browsing
2. Meaning over volume
3. Explanation over black-box matching
4. Calm elegance over gamification
5. Trust and safety by structure
6. 사람을 관계 이름으로 먼저 규정하지 않는다

## What WENET is NOT
- 외형 중심 소비형 스와이프 앱
- 도파민 리워드 앱
- 관계를 한 종류로만 강요하는 앱

## Core promise
For each recommendation, WENET must answer:
- Why this connection may fit
- Which relationship direction is most plausible now (friend/peer first)
- What caution points exist

## Safety posture
- Identity verification + age verification are first-class entities
- Minor/adult pools are structurally isolated
- Discovery eligibility and trust score gate exposure
- Gradual connection flow minimizes oversharing

## UX tone
- Quiet, elegant, low-noise
- Fewer but meaningful recommendations
- Explanations readable by non-technical users
